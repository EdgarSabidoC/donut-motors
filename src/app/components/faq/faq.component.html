Once the inspection is scheduled, the time it takes to sell your car depends on how quickly you send us the requested documents. Therefore, it is important that you check the list of documents in your email.
Remember that the validity of the offer is 7 days, although more than half of the cars we buy on the same day of the inspection.

Selling your car is very simple. The steps you must follow are:

1. Get a quote for your car at Donut Motors.
2. Schedule an inspection appointment at one of our locations.
3. Send the required documents.
4. Receive the validated purchase offer, accept it, and receive payment for your car.

Check our website for our locations and business hours.
